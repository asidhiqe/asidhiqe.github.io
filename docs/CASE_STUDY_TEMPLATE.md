# Principal UX/UI Case Study Blueprint & Framework

This document defines the authoritative, enterprise-grade case study template for Aboobacker Sidhiqe's portfolio. Every case study must teach a design lesson—not just present a project.

---

## Complete 13-Section Case Study Structure

### 1. Overview & Hook
- One paragraph setting the context: domain, core problem, and the primary design lesson.
- Hook the reader immediately with operational stakes.

### 2. The Problem & Business Context
- State the problem from the user's operational reality and the business consequence.
- Why existing tools or spreadsheets failed under operational load.

### 3. My Role & Stakeholder Alignment
- Specific scope of ownership: decisions made, team alignment, executive diplomacy.
- How conflicting engineering, product, and compliance requirements were bridged.

### 4. User Archetypes & Behavioral Mental Models (Personas)
- Role-based archetypes focusing strictly on operational goals, friction points, and risk tolerance.
- Avoid generic stock demographic templates.

### 5. Constraints, Accessibility & Localization
- Technical, timeline, legacy system, or regulatory constraints (HIPAA, HL7, SOC2).
- Internationalization & RTL Arabic localization details where applicable.
- Accessibility standards (WCAG 2.1 AA/AAA compliance).

### 6. Information Architecture (IA) & User Flows
- High-level system navigation structure and task flows.
- Before vs. After IA maps showing consolidation of complex multi-step tasks.

### 7. Design System & Component Architecture
- Custom enterprise design tokens (color palette, typography scale, elevation, density).
- High-density components created (Data tables, status chips, side-drawers, JSON trace logs).

### 8. Brand Identity & Visual Assets *(If Applicable)*
- Visual identity construction: logo mark design, color palette logic, typography selection.
- Marketing collateral: pitch decks, brochures, trade show graphics, and rollout assets.

### 9. Core Product Flows & Solution Showcase
- Key interaction flows framed as decisions made rather than static UI features.
- Step-by-step walkthrough of primary operational screens.

### 10. Principal Design Decisions & Trade-off Matrix
Highlight 2-3 critical design decisions formatted as:
> **Decision:** [What was decided]  
> **Context:** [Why this decision mattered]  
> **Alternatives:** [What else was considered]  
> **Rationale:** [Why this option won]  
> **Tradeoff:** [What was sacrificed]

### 11. Testing & Engineering Handoff Governance
- Usability testing protocols, task completion validation, and prototype reviews.
- Design QA process, Figma token syncing, empty state, loading, and error handling.

### 12. Verified Impact & Qualitative Outcomes
- Defensible qualitative or quantitative impact (NDA safe).
- Adoption signals, user feedback, and workflow efficiency improvements.

### 13. Reflection & What I'd Change Today
- Intellectual honesty on lessons learned and what would be redesigned with today's technology.
