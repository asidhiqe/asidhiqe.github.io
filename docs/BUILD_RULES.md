# Build Rules

These rules override implementation convenience.

## Rule 1

Story before visuals.

---

## Rule 2

Content before components.

---

## Rule 3

Information Architecture before layout.

---

## Rule 4

Meaning before motion.

---

## Rule 5

Every section must answer

Why does this exist?

---

## Rule 6

Every animation must improve understanding.

---

## Rule 7

Every sentence must be interview-defensible.

Never invent

- metrics
- percentages
- scale
- users
- integrations

---

## Rule 8

Prefer observations over claims.

---

## Rule 9

Avoid generic Product Design language.

Never use

- passionate
- user-centric
- innovative
- pixel perfect
- problem solver
- design thinker

---

## Rule 10

Every project must teach.

Not merely present.

---

## Rule 11

Every page must reinforce the manifesto.

---

## Rule 12

The portfolio is evaluated using

Trust

↓

Credibility

↓

Clarity

↓

Leadership

↓

Systems Thinking

↓

Memorability

Visual polish comes last.
